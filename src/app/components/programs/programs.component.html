Our Programmes

Digital Transformation

Y4D utilises ICT in education to broaden content outreach and delivery methods with the intention of informing more young people. Y4D designs different interactive channels, platforms and tools where youth talk about important topics of their interest. Y4D creates a space to share good practices with the intention of consolidating the youth community.

Women & Youth Empowerment

Y4D promotes capacity building activities through vocational training and resource centres to vulnerable youths with income generation activities such as dress-making, hair-dressing, beads-making and table-banking projects.

Ensuring Food Security

Y4D works to improve food security and ensure sustainable livelihoods programs using integrated interventions focused on sustainable agricultural projects such as poultry, fish, rabbits and cattle farming.

Combating Climate Change

Environmental education programs with youth groups such as tree planting projects; advocating for SMART agricultural practices in reducing the negative effects of global warming through vegetables and fruits farming.

Children, Gender, & HIV/AIDS Program

Y4D has been involved in awareness creation, sensitization, and behavioral change advocacy focusing on vulnerable groups such as women, children and youth.

Early Childhood Education

Y4D is passionate about making sure that all children including disabled have access to essential care and promote better education and development outcomes for the young people.

Youth Extracurricular Activities

Y4D groups involves the youth in extracurricular activities such as sports, cultural festivals, camps, music and drama among others.
